# Vegas Blitz Proposed Core Test Cases

Source basis: Planner outputs, DOCX game rules, parsheet data, oracle workbook notes, and the repository example window-test format.

These are static full-window evaluator tests. They are not reel-stop tests because stop-index semantics, wrap direction, and visible-row ordering are unresolved.

Final currency amounts are not asserted for standard ways or SCATTER wins until payout-unit and denomination rules are clarified. COR expectations use x total bet because that unit is specified.

Symbols use Planner labels: HV1-HV5, LV1-LV6, WILD, SCATTER, COR, COLLECT.

## TC-001 Pay Window Shape

MODE: Base
PURPOSE: Verify evaluator accepts a 6 reel x 4 row visible window.

FULL_WINDOW:
row 0 | HV1 | HV2 | HV3 | HV4 | HV5 | LV1
row 1 | LV2 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV2
row 2 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV3 | HV4
row 3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV5 | LV1 | LV2

ACTIVE_WINDOW:
row 0 | HV1 | HV2 | HV3 | HV4 | HV5 | LV1
row 1 | LV2 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV2
row 2 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV3 | HV4
row 3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV5 | LV1 | LV2

EXPECTED:
- Window dimensions = 6 reels x 4 rows.
- SCATTER count = 0.
- COLLECT count = 0.
- No feature trigger.
