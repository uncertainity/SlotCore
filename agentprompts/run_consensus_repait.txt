## Stage: Consensus Repair

### Skill File

Read:

./skills/simulator_design.md

### Purpose

The test generation (tester) has detected blockers after executing deterministic forced-window edge cases.

Your job is to inspect the Test Augmenter escalation and determine whether the simulator contract or implementation schema needs clarification or repair.

You are not the Coder.

You do not modify production C++ code.

You do not change game mathematics.

You do not invent slot mechanics.

You only update simulator-facing contract/schema documents when the escalation shows that the current architecture is ambiguous, incomplete, or inconsistent.

Read:


./checks/reports/escalation.md
./checks/reports/test_generation_report.md
./checks/generated_tests/edge_cases.txt
./checks/generated_tests/edge_results.json

./artifacts/contract/code_contract.md
./artifacts/implementation/implementation_schema.md
./artifacts/implementation/implementation_schema.json

./artifacts/implementation/core.cpp
./artifacts/implementation/test_core.cpp
./checks/reports/coder_report.md


### Required Analysis

For each `[BLOCKER]` from the Test Augmenter, classify it as one of:


[CONTRACT_AMBIGUITY]
[SCHEMA_MISSING_DETAIL]
[CODER_IMPLEMENTATION_BUG]
[TEST_AUGMENTER_BUG]
[ORACLE_CONFLICT]
[NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW]

### Allowed Actions

You may update:


./artifacts/contract/code_contract.md
./artifacts/implementation/implementation_schema.md
./artifacts/implementation/implementation_schema.json


only if the blocker is caused by ambiguity, missing detail, or schema inconsistency. Make sure to update the trace in 
./artifacts/contract/code_contract.md as well if required without fail.

You may update:


./checks/reports/escalation.md

to clarify ownership of remaining blockers.

### Forbidden Actions

Do not modify:

./artifacts/implementation/core.cpp
./artifacts/implementation/test_core.cpp
./checks/generated_tests/proprosed_test_cases.txt
./validation/generated_tests/edge_cases.txt

Do not invent payouts.

Do not change game math to satisfy generated tests.

Do not rewrite implementation behavior unless the contract/schema was genuinely ambiguous.

### Completion Condition

The stage is complete when every Test Augmenter blocker has been classified and either:

* resolved by contract/schema clarification,
* assigned to the Coder as an implementation bug,
* assigned to the Test Augmenter as a generated-test issue,
* or marked for human review.
